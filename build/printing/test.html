title: “Darwin’s Orchid” summary: “The great scientist’s bad day.” thumbnail: darwin-1.jpg weight: 91

In 1860, Charle’s Darwin had a bad day:

I am very poorly today & very stupid & hate everybody & everything. One lives only to make blunders.

I am going to write a little Book for Murray on orchids & today I hate them worse than everything.

Charles Darwin, Letter to Charles Lytrell, 1860

This broadside serves to inspire

Darwin’s grumpy letter to friend seemed inspiring enough to justify a broadside that I put together in the bosom of winter in 2014.

Poor fellow, he hates them worse than anything!

Set in Palintino and printed by the Puritan Press on 100lb eggshell. Edition of 75.
