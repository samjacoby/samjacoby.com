As of this writing, it's a 41 year-old machine, and requires continuous attention. That is part of the fun. I've found great satisfaction in working : tracing down bad electrical connections, adjusting the tappets, changing the tires, my hands dabbed with oil, inhaling petroleum's intoxicating bouquet.
