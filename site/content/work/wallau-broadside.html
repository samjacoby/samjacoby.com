--- author: Rudolf Koch title: Wallau Broadside summary: "A typespecimen from Klingspor originals." date: 2012-08-18 16:25:31 thumbnail: 'img/thumb_wallau-2.jpg' --- This broadside was printed in Mainz, Germany, at the _Druckladen des Gutenberg Museums_. It's Rudolf Koch's Wallau, a _rundgotisch_ typeface, with elements of classic German blackletter and traditional roman capitals.
