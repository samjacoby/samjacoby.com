--- author: Rudolf Koch title: Wallau Broadside summary: "A type specimen printed from Klingspor originals." weight: 15 thumbnail: wallau-1.jpg --- I printed this broadside in Mainz, Germany, at the _Druckladen des Gutenberg Museums_, while I was a _praktikant_ there. It's Rudolf Koch's Wallau, a _rundgotisch_ typeface, with elements of classic German blackletter and traditional roman capitals. Dan Reynolds has a whole lot more information [here](http://www.typeoff.de/2005/06/17/die-wallau-schriftprobe/).
